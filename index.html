Services

Everything your skis need.

From quick touch-ups to full overhauls, we keep your gear performing on hardpack, powder, and spring slush.

Performance Prep Ski Tune

Advanced start-of-season tune designed to set a precise edge structure, true the base, and fully prep skis for peak performance from day one.

Full Ski Tune-Up

A complete tune to restore edge hold, glide, and overall performance. Ideal for mid-season refreshes or skis that no longer feel consistent on snow.

Full Snowboard Tune-Up

Comprehensive service to improve control, speed, and feel. Best for boards that feel slow, unstable, or less responsive.

Performance Prep Snowboard Tune

Advanced start-of-season service focused on dialing in edges and base structure for smooth, predictable performance all season.

Hot Base Wax

Deep hot wax treatment to restore base moisture, improve glide, and protect against wear. Recommended regularly throughout the season.

Precision Edge Sharpening

Carefully sharpened edges for improved grip and confidence on firm and icy conditions. Clean, consistent results without over-grinding.

Edge Maintenance Tune

Light edge maintenance using diamond stones to clean, smooth, and restore sharpness. Perfectfor maintaining consistent edge feel.

P-Tex Base Repair

Repair of base damage and core shots to extend equipment life and prevent further deterioration.

Binding Adjustment

Professional binding adjustment to ensure proper fit, function, and safe release based on your setup.
